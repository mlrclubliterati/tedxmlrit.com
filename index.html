TEDxMLRIT is a local, independently organized event to be conducted at MLR Institute of Technology, Hyderabad. It strives to re-create the unique experience found at TED, where the world’s leading thinkers and doers congregate to share what they are most passionate about.


At its core, the fundamental goal of TED and TEDxMLRIT is to foster and spread great ideas. We aim to provide a platform where the smartest thinkers, greatest visionaries, and most fascinating teachers will be inspired and will have the opportunity to inspire others.


TEDxMLRIT will throw light on the epitome of knowledge which would fill in the grid of ideas.We shall provide ourselves into getting One Step Closer to you as a means of nourishing your soul.






Our theme ‘One Step Closer’, is all about breaking the stereotypes and reaching new heights. Stereotypes become too rigid and wouldn’t give any room to growth. To break out of an entrenched stereotype is not easy. Part of it involves knowing or researching what others believed us to be in the past, what they believe now and what they see for the future. And we must do it systematically, by specific audiences that are important to us. E.g. college students, clergy, single career women, the business community etc...

When people rely on surface appearances, rather than in-depth knowledge of others at the level of the heart, mind and spirit, their ability to assess and understand people accurately is compromised. They are clichés, and they present a human being as far more simple and uniform than any human being actually is. We found speakers who enhance the TED experience and immerse the audience in their own form of idea worth spreading. #onestepcloser.






An inspiring talk can motivate you to reach for the stars or even change your life. This year TEDxMLRIT will host a variety of speakers travelling within Hyderabad and from abroad making the event the ideal place to “plant a seed” that will inspire ideas and change.
